Welcome

Atlas is a cross-platform design system that gives teams the foundation they need to build engaging, high-quality digital products and experiences with Mendix.

Principles

Atlas is designed and measured by a set of guiding principles that define our goals and values.

  • Atlas is empowering.

    Enabling users to create amazing products and experiences that look and feel best-in-class.

  • Atlas is elegant.

    Its design language allows users to create modern and sophisticated experiences.

  • Atlas is personal.

    Users can easily tailor experiences to embody the look and feel of their brand identity.

  • Atlas is consistent.

    Create experiences that are consistent across platforms, yet still feel natural to the device being used.

  • Atlas is flexible.

    Its components are designed to work seamlessly with each other, in whichever combination suits the needs of the user.

  • Atlas is tangible.

    Typography, alignment, color, depth and motion convey a sense of hierarchy and structure to users.

Typography

Atlas uses system fonts. This ensures that typography is optimised for performance, accessibility, compatibility and modern features such as HiDPI screens and dynamic font rendering. To ensure your product experience remains consistent and elegant across platforms, we use the same type scale (font size & line height) regardless of platform. This set of font styles is based on a middle-ground between default font styles for Android and iOS, aligned to a major second (1.125) type scale. Font size and line height values where rounded off to fit our 2x grid philosophy of division.

Color

Atlas uses its colour scheme to communicate structure and brand identity within a user interface. Usage and variance of colour is kept minimal to reduce sensory overload and help the user get to where they need to go as fast as possible. Greyscale variances help communicate a sense of content hierarchy to the user, with colour used to highlight call-to-actions and important information, along with establishing a sense of brand identity. A guiding principle for Atlas is to reduce visual friction by keeping contrast levels low while keeping primary interface assets accessible. A dark theme is used to allow products to be switched from a lighter to a darker colour scheme on the fly, optimising user experiences across all light levels.

Text

Text is displayed in pure black or pure white, depending on the surface. Different levels of opacity are used to communicate a sense of typographical hierarchy to users; the lower the opacity, the lower priority. Pure white and black with lowered opacity was chosen over fixed greyscale colours to ensure text blends well with a wide variety of background colours and images.

Branding

Atlas uses a variation of the Mendix Ultramarine colour as a placeholder for a primary brand colour. Mendix developers are free to alter this colour to fit their brand guidelines. Meta colours, specifically for success, warning and danger, are preconfigured and can be edited at will.

Spacing

Individual object sizing, margin and padding measurements are defined by multiples of 24. The baseline is set at 16px and size variables throughout Atlas represent a division or multiplication of this.
